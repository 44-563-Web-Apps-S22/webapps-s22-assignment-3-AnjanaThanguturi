Bawarchi Indian Cuisine, Kansas City

Bawarchi is famous for Authentic Indian Biryanis, in which aromatic indian spices will be added together in order to create tantalizing pot biryani. If you are looking to eat delicious Indian food, Bawarchi will be your first place. This will be your home away from home. There are some cool attractions near Bawarchi such as National WWI Musuem and Memorial, Kansas City zoo, The National Musuem of Toys and Miniatures.
