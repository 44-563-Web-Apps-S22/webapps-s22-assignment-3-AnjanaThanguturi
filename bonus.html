Toledo Thunders

Toledo Thor is the mascot of the Northwest Missouri State University for Football. It is designed by Anjana Thanguturi. The mascot Thunders is an alligator which is in green and yellow color.

Fun facts about Alligator:

  • Alligators continue to grow throughout their lifetimes.
